FAQs — buying on WhatsApp

Simple answers for first-time buyers and subscription customers.

WhatsApp-first ordering
How do I place an order on WhatsApp?

Click any WhatsApp button on the website. Your message will open with the product name already filled in. Send your name, address, city, and preferred delivery day.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are recurring supply plans based on weekly egg quantities. You confirm your plan on WhatsApp, and delivery timing is agreed directly with you before dispatch.

Can I order one-time packs first and subscribe later?

Yes. Many buyers start with one-time packs before moving to a weekly or monthly plan.

What if poultry is out of stock?

You can still message on WhatsApp to register interest. Siran Valley can update you when the next batch becomes available.

Will online payment be added later?

Yes. The website structure is ready for future payment integration when you decide to move beyond WhatsApp-first ordering.

Do prices stay fixed?

Prices shown on the website are current display prices and may be updated over time based on supply, feed, packaging, delivery, or promotional offers.

Terms & conditions

Simple customer-facing terms designed for WhatsApp orders, subscriptions, and future scale-up.

For website & WhatsApp sales
These terms are written for your current operation: WhatsApp-first ordering, manual confirmation, displayed website prices, out-of-stock poultry, and future payment integration.
1. Orders
All orders placed through the website are completed through WhatsApp confirmation. An order is considered confirmed only after Siran Valley Organic Farm acknowledges it directly.
2. Product availability
Egg packs, subscriptions, poultry, and meat offerings are subject to availability. Poultry listings marked out of stock are for inquiry and future reservation interest only.
3. Pricing
Prices displayed on the website are current selling prices and may change without prior notice. Promotional discounts may be introduced later and will apply only when clearly displayed.
4. Subscriptions
Subscription plans are recurring supply arrangements agreed directly on WhatsApp. Frequency, quantity, and delivery arrangements are confirmed with each customer. Changes or pauses should be communicated in advance.
5. Delivery
Delivery areas, delivery days, and delivery charges may vary by location. Customers should provide accurate delivery information to avoid delays.
6. Cancellations and changes
For one-time orders, customers should notify Siran Valley as early as possible if they wish to cancel or change an order. For subscriptions, notice should be given before the next dispatch cycle.
7. Product handling
Eggs and poultry should be checked upon receipt. Eggs should be stored appropriately after delivery. Poultry buyers are responsible for proper handling, housing, feeding, and care after collection or delivery.
8. Website information
Product images are used to represent style, packaging, and product category. Actual appearance may vary slightly by batch, season, age, or packaging updates.
9. Future payments
Online payment methods may be added later. Until then, payment arrangements are handled directly during order confirmation.
10. Contact
For any order, subscription, stock, or delivery query, customers should contact Siran Valley Organic Farm on WhatsApp using the buttons provided on this website.
