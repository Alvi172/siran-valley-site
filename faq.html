Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed answers covering ordering, subscriptions, delivery, poultry enquiries, product quality, and customer support.

Ordering & WhatsApp

How do I place an order?

Click any WhatsApp button on the website. A message opens with the product already mentioned. Send your name, phone number, address, city, preferred delivery day, quantity, and any notes.

Can I ask questions before ordering?

Yes. Customers can message first for availability, pricing confirmation, product photos, poultry enquiries, or delivery guidance before placing a final order.

Do I need to make an account?

No. The website is designed for simple direct ordering without account creation. WhatsApp is the main ordering and support channel.

How do I know my order is confirmed?

An order is confirmed only after Siran Valley Organic Farm replies directly on WhatsApp and acknowledges your order details.

Subscriptions

How do egg subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are weekly supply plans based on the selected egg quantity. Customers choose a plan, confirm details on WhatsApp, and delivery timing is arranged directly.

Can I pause or change a subscription later?

Yes. Customers should message in advance before the next dispatch cycle if they want to pause, change, or update their subscription arrangement.

Can I start with one-time packs and subscribe later?

Yes. Many customers begin with one-time egg packs and then move to a regular plan once they are satisfied with the quality and service.

Will discounts always be available?

No. Discount offers may be introduced at selected times only. Unless a discount is clearly shown, the standard listed price applies.

Delivery & Location

Do you deliver only in Mansehra?

The website is mainly positioned around Mansehra orders. Customers in other cities can still message on WhatsApp to ask about possible delivery options.

How are delivery days decided?

Delivery timing is arranged directly with each customer depending on product type, route planning, subscription timing, and current availability.

Can delivery charges apply?

Yes. Depending on the order size, location, and route, a delivery charge may apply. This is confirmed during order coordination.

Poultry Enquiries

What if poultry is marked out of stock?

You can still message on WhatsApp to register your interest. This helps Siran Valley update you when the next batch is available.

Can I ask for current pictures or videos?

Yes. Poultry buyers can request recent photos, age confirmation, and availability details through WhatsApp before proceeding.

Can I ask for bulk or custom poultry batches?

Yes. If you want larger quantities, a future batch booking, or a specific age category, you can send a custom enquiry directly on WhatsApp.

Product Quality

What makes Siran Valley different?

The brand is positioned around clean valley-based rearing, fresher air, natural surroundings, direct farm trust, and simpler household-focused service away from the stress and pollution of larger cities.

Can products vary slightly by batch?

Yes. Egg size, shell appearance, packaging style, and poultry appearance can vary slightly by batch, season, and age.

Can prices change later?

Yes. Displayed prices may be updated over time based on supply conditions, feed costs, packaging, transport, or special offers.
